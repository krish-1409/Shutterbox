Get Started

We are a diverse club of amateurs with mobile cameras to professionals with DSLRs, all sharing the same passion for photography. You will find us at odd nook and corners of the campus at unexpected times, shooting sunset at the lakeside, making light graffitis in the dark of night, or capturing the beauties of nature!

Our activities and works include:

  • Event Photography
  • Photowalks
  • Technical WorkShops by industry experts
  • Beginner Sessions
  • Editing Sessions
  • Film Making
  • Media and Public Relations Department of Fests
  • Photography Competetions
  • Quizzes
  • Photography Trips
